about us

STEMBoard creates leading-edge technologies that help advance our nation and its citizens

AT A Glance

STEMBoard’s leadership team has engineering and development experience in creating smart systems and software solutions for defense and enterprise clients. STEMBoard is a certified Small Disadvantaged Businesses and a Economically Disadvantaged Woman-Owned Small Business.

MISSION

STEMBoard creates leading-edge technologies that help advance our nation and its citizens. We do this by educating and developing 21st-century professionals to further equip them to meet this challenge.

VISION

STEMBoard seeks to improve society through systematic innovation by attracting, nurturing, and developing the world’s emerging brilliance.

VALUES

Integrity & Innovation: We deliver the high-quality solutions we promise, on time and on budget. We demand technical excellence in our products, our team and our partners.
